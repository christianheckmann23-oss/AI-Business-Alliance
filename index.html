About AIBA

Our Mission

The AI in Business Alliance (AIBA) is dedicated to bridging the gap between emerging AI technologies and real-world business applications. We empower business students with AI literacy and hands-on experience to become innovative leaders in their fields.

Our Vision

To establish Ohio University as a hub for AI-driven business innovation, creating lasting impact on members by enhancing their career readiness and promoting OU's core values of leadership and ethical practice.

What Makes Us Different

AIBA combines structured learning, practical projects, and industry engagement. We focus exclusively on business applications of AI, complementing existing academic offerings and filling a unique gap in student organizations at OU.

Our Functional Teams

Finance Team

Exploring AI in finance and fintech, including algorithmic trading, fraud detection, credit scoring, and risk management.

Healthcare Team

Focusing on AI-driven medical diagnostics, patient data management, and drug discovery acceleration using machine learning.

Education Team

Examining AI in education technology, personalized learning systems, and intelligent tutoring platforms.

Marketing/Retail Team

Looking at AI in marketing and retail, including customer insights, dynamic pricing, and recommendation engines.

Supply Chain Team

Covering AI in supply chain management, demand forecasting, inventory optimization, and logistics routing.

Join AIBA

Membership Requirements

  • Open to all Ohio University undergraduates
  • Brief application and casual interview
  • Attend 75% of club events per semester
  • Contribute to at least one project team
  • Maintain professional conduct

What You'll Gain

  • Hands-on AI experience in business contexts
  • Industry connections and mentorship
  • Portfolio-worthy projects
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Career preparation and networking

Application Process

Submit your application through our online form, followed by a brief interview with an executive member. We're looking for passionate students ready to explore the intersection of AI and business.
